Hands Up - Crime Portal Management System

About

HandsUp is an online crime portal management system highly helpful to handle all the crimes and illegal activities raised among any individual at their institute.

Why ?

Crimes are raised in day by day. It is not only happened in cities or villages also happened in all institutions for women and men. The main reason is that no one have guts to complain to any one regarding their problems. So to overcome that problem it is good to have this portal system.

The Right Place

This is the right place for all students and faculties to complain their problems and make their life freedom. This is the right place for all to ensure their freedom in their working place.
